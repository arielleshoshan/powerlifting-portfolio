About

Are you a few months or a few years into your powerlifting journey?

The purpose of this website is to function as a guide that is quick and easy to use for powerlifting geared specifically for rookie or amateur lifters. So any powerlifter can quickly learn and access their 1 Rep Max (1RM) by using the calculator, have a workout returned based on the their muscle group selection, access federation information, a top powerlifting coach, and brief pictures of my competition and experience with the sport!

These exercises with set/rep ranges, including RPE, were picked by Jon Mead, a knowledgeable powerlifting coach who competes in powerlifting and strongman competitions. The workouts are generalized but the most impactful movements in a powerlifting-focused program.

Federations

There are two main federations in Powerlifting. There are several other smaller recognized federations, but USAPL and IPF are the most popular of the 15. The difference between both federations is the drug tests and lift rules. USAPL drug tests their athletes on every level, whereas IPF drug tests only at a national level.

USAPL uses one type of barbell. Other organizations will use specialty bars depending on the lift. IPF is changing its bench rules due to athletes taking extreme measures to reduce their range of motion. Effective January 1, 2023, IPF will judge the bench press under strict criteria.

Competition

I got into powerlifting years ago and was in a terrible car accident resulting in the slipped discs of my L4 and L5 lower lumbar spine. I was unable to powerlift for multiple years. For the last 18 weeks, I found an incredible coach who worked with me on my injuries and tailored a program to my powerlifting goals. You can be like me, too, using the right coach and proper form to achieve your SBD dream maxes! This is the beginning of a comeback story for my fitness experience, and I couldn't be happier.

Representing UF Gator Powerlifting, I competed at the USAPL Collegiate States Powerlifting Competition in the 67.5kg weight class and had a toal of 545.
